# Brehm Consulting Website

A professional consulting website for Brehm Consulting, a management consultancy specializing in sales, marketing, and business management for startups in the hospitality and out-of-home (OOH) markets.

## Project Overview

This website showcases Brehm Consulting's expertise and services, targeting startups and mid-sized companies in Germany and Switzerland, particularly in the hospitality, food service, and tech sectors.

## Key Features

- **SEO-Optimized**: Comprehensive metadata, semantic HTML, and keyword-rich content for German/Swiss search markets
- **Professional Design**: Modern, clean design inspired by the existing Brehm Consulting brand identity
- **Brand Colors**: 
  - Navy Blue (#435670) - Primary brand color
  - Cyan (#00BCD4) - Secondary/action color
  - Coral (#F4A582) - Accent color
  - Light Blue (#A8DADC) - Supporting color
- **Responsive Layout**: Mobile-first design that works seamlessly across all devices
- **Service Showcase**: Four core competency areas (Hospitality, StartUps, Marketing, Sales)
- **Contact Integration**: Direct phone and email contact options

## Pages & Sections

### Homepage (/)
- **Hero Section**: Strong value proposition with nautical wave graphics reflecting the brand's navigation theme
- **Value Proposition**: Clear messaging about growth strategies for the out-of-home market
- **Services Grid**: Four core competencies with icons and descriptions
- **Expertise Details**: Three-step approach to scaling businesses
- **Contact CTA**: Call-to-action with contact information

### Marketing Page (/marketing)
- **Hero Section**: Compelling headline about marketing services for startups with data analytics imagery
- **Value Proposition**: Three key benefits (Precise targeting, measurable results, sustainable growth)
- **Detailed Services**: 
  - Analysis & Status Quo: Marketing audit and optimization roadmap
  - Marketing Strategy Development: Market analysis, automation, AI-driven lead generation
  - Digital Marketing & Social Media: SEO/GEO, SEA, social media strategies
  - Marketing Automation & CRM: Salesforce/HubSpot integration, email campaigns
  - Content Creation: Blog articles, website texts, social media content
  - Press Relations: Press releases, journalist network, press events
- **Why Brehm Consulting**: 30+ years experience, startup focus, measurable results, holistic approach
- **CTA Section**: Free initial consultation offer with contact details
- **SEO Optimized**: Keywords targeting German/Swiss market for marketing consulting, lead generation, and performance marketing

### Sales & Marketing Wissen (/wissen)
- **Knowledge Base**: 20 comprehensive Q&A articles covering critical sales and marketing topics
- **Simplified Layout**: Text-only cards with emoji icons inspired by modern landing page design
- **Single Grid View**: All articles displayed in one cohesive grid (no category separation)
- **Clean Hero**: Badge, title, and subtitle without background image for fast loading
- **Info Box**: Descriptive section explaining the knowledge base purpose
- **SEO Optimized**: Targets B2B sales professionals seeking actionable insights with geo-specific keywords for Germany and Switzerland
- **Individual Article Pages**: Each question has its own detailed 2000-3000 word article with frameworks and examples

### Blog (/blog)
- **Best Practices & Case Studies**: Real client projects, competitor analysis, and implementation stories
- **Current Articles**: 6 detailed posts covering CRM implementation, website optimization, and consulting projects
- **Categories**: Best Practice, Kunden (Customers), Management
- **SEO Optimized**: Geographic targeting for German and Swiss markets with practice-focused keywords
- **Improved UX**: Cleaner card design with better visual hierarchy, no date display to emphasize evergreen content value

## Target Audience

- Startups in the hospitality and food service sector
- Mid-sized companies in the out-of-home (OOH) market
- Tech companies serving the hospitality industry
- Businesses in Germany and Switzerland seeking growth consulting

## Brand Identity

- **Logo**: Blue-orange gradient "B" symbol
- **Tagline**: "Kurs halten und erfolgreich navigieren" (Stay on course and navigate successfully)
- **Theme**: Nautical/navigation metaphor for business consulting
- **Tone**: Professional, trustworthy, expertise-driven

## Technical Stack

- **Framework**: Next.js with App Router
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS with custom brand colors
- **Components**: shadcn/ui component library
- **Typography**: System fonts for fast loading
- **Images**: High-quality stock photos from Pexels + brand logos
